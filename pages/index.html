Our Company

Pearl City Marine Products Pvt. Ltd was established in 2015, by a group of professionals who are stalwarts in the business of manufacturing of fishmeal and fish oil. We manufacture superior quality Fish Meal and Fish Oil in our modern and fully mechanised state-of-the-art Fish Meal and Fish Oil plant situated on the coast of Tuticorin, the heart of fisheries industry in South India. We are serving best quality products over the years and have established ourselves as a popular brand in the international as well as national market.
